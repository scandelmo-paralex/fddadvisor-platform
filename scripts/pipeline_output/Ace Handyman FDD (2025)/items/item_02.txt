William Guzik – Director
William Guzik has been our Director since September 2019. He is the Executive Vice President, Chief
Financial Officer and Chief Risk Officer for Ace Hardware, positions he has held since January 2014.
Between November 2021 and June 2023, he served as the Vice President, Finance, of APF. He served in
a similar capacity for other entities owned by Ace Hardware that have since been merged into AHHS.
Mr. Guzik is a Director of AHHS, a position he has held since June 7, 2023. Mr. Guzik is located at the
headquarters of Ace Hardware in Oak Brook, Illinois.
Andy Bell – Chief Executive Officer and Founder
Andy Bell is the founder of the Ace Handyman Services System. He currently serves as our Chief
Executive Officer, and has held the positions of Chief Executive Officer, President, or Director at various
times since our inception in 2000 to September 2019. He also serves as Vice President, Ace Hardware
Home Services – Ace Handyman Services Division of AHHS, a position he has held since June 2023. He
served in similar positions for our former operating entities that have merged into AHHS. Since
December 2021, Mr. Bell has been the Chief Executive Officer of APF. Since July 2023, he also serves
as a Manager of Bell Franchising, LLC, located in Lakewood, Colorado. Mr. Bell also served on the
Board of Directors of Tetras Properties, LLC located in Lakewood, Colorado from March 2005 until it
was dissolved in September 2023. Mr. Bell retains management responsibility relating to the sale and
operation of franchises offered under this Disclosure Document. He is located in our offices in
Lakewood, Colorado.
Chris Bue – President
Chris Bue has been employed by us since September 2014, first as our Chief Operating Officer, and from
September 2018 to September 2019 as our Chief Executive Officer, and currently as our President. From
September 2019 to June 2023, he served in similar positions for our former operating entities that have
merged into AHHS. Mr. Bue is located in our offices in Lakewood, Colorado.
Colette Bell – Vice President of Franchise Development
Colette Bell has been employed as Vice President of Franchise Development for us since January 2019.
From September 2019 to June 2023, she served in similar positions for our former operating entities that
have merged into AHHS. Since July 2023, Ms. Bell also serves as a Manager of Bell Franchising, LLC.
Ms. Bell is located in our offices in Lakewood, Colorado. Since December 2021, she has also been Vice
President, Franchise Development for APF.
LaShelle L. Taylor – Vice President of Franchise Operations
LaShelle Taylor has been our Vice President of Franchise Operations since August 2021. She served as
our Franchise Consultant from March 2021 to August 2021. Ms. Taylor is located in our offices in

Karen T. Bloomfield – Vice President, Marketing
Karen Bloomfield has been our Vice President, Marketing since September 2023. She has also served in
the same position for AHHS and APF since September 2023. Ms. Bloomfield previously was engaged by
Ace Hardware as Director of Merchandising Management - Ecommerce from January 2022 to August

Jeff A. Klos – Vice President, Finance
Jeff Klos has been our Vice President, Finance since June 2023. He has served in the same position for
APF since June 2023. Mr. Klos has been employed by Ace Hardware since April 2000 and currently
serves as Vice President Finance, Ace Hardware Home Services (since June 2023). Mr. Klos has
previously held the positions of Corporate Vice President, Financial Planning and Analysis and Strategy,
from July 2020 to May 2023; and Director, Financial Planning and Analysis from March 2017 to June
2020. Mr. Klos is located in Oak Brook, Illinois.
