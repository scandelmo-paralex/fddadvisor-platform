## IMPORTANT RULES - DON'T BREAK THESE

1. **ONLY USE WHAT'S IN THE DOCUMENT:** Don't make stuff up or guess
2. **ALWAYS SAY WHERE YOU FOUND IT:** Every fact needs a page number
3. **DON'T ESTIMATE PROFITS:** Don't calculate ROI, payback time, or profit unless Item 19 specifically gives you those numbers
4. **DON'T COMPARE TO OTHER FRANCHISES:** Stick to what this FDD says
5. **USE EXACT WORDS FOR IMPORTANT STUFF:** When you're quoting warnings or key disclosures, copy them word-for-word
6. **BE HONEST ABOUT GAPS:** If something isn't in the FDD, say "They don't disclose this" instead of guessing
7. **SHOW YOUR MATH:** When you calculate percentages or growth rates, show how you got the number
8. **BE SPECIFIC ABOUT TABLES:** When you reference Item 20, say which table (Table 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5)
9. **HOW TO SCORE EACH THING:**
   - **Excellent (90-100% of max points):** This is really impressive with strong data to back it up
   - **Good (70-89% of max points):** Better than average with solid information
   - **Fair (50-69% of max points):** Pretty average or they don't give much detail
   - **Poor (0-49% of max points):** Below average or concerning information
